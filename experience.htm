Research

I completed a fourth year thesis project based on data collected on the Bliss Project. My thesis was titled Calculating the total biomass of breeding amphibians at Bat Lake, Algonquin Park. The poster created was awarded honorable mention in the Undergraduate Poster Fair, Category - Life history ecology and evolution

Field Work

Saving Turtles at Risk Today, START (Scales Nature Park)
Seasonal Field Technician
In this position I planned and led reptile and amphibian surveys within remote areas of central Ontario, Canada with an emphasis on species at risk. This position required I identify and collect spatial, morphometric, DNA data from a variety of reptile and amphibian species. Additionally I Conducted road mortality and nesting surveys: road cruised for nesting females, caged suitable nests, collected high risk nests forartificial incubation.
Georgian Bay Conservation Action, Research and Education about Snakes (C.A.R.E.S.)
Seasonal Field Technician
In this position I planned and led hibernation and gestation surveys for Eastern Massasauga Rattlesnakes. Captured and took morphometric and DNA data from adults and neonates.
Sakaerat Naja Project (Suranaree University of Technology)
Field Technician 6-month internship
Using radio telemetry tracked the movement of Naja kaouthia and Naja Siamensis Nakhon Ratchasima District, Thailand. Prepared weekly reports of individuals bi-daily movements. Conducted prey surveys for small mammals, amphibians and reptiles using drift fences and Sherman traps. Able to identify 60+ snake species within Thailand. Bat Lake Amphibian Surveys, BLISS (University of Toronto) Seasonal Field Technician Performed drift fence and aquatic minnow trap surveys for a variety of amphibians over the 2018 breeding season. Took morphometric measurements and DNA samples were collected from individuals.
Small Mammal Project, (Laurentian University) Seasonal Field Technician
Trapped small mammal species using Longworth, Tomahawk and Sherman traps. Animal handling included ear tagging individuals, collecting parasites, collection of fecal samples in addition to relevant morphological data of each individual.

About

Hi my name is Natalia. I am a current student at Fleming College. I am a 2019 graduate from the University of Toronto. I am passionate about wildlife, conservation and in particular I enjoy the study of reptiles and amphibians. I am confident in my ability to work long hours in a variety of environments including in hot/cold temperatures, rainy conditions and in the presence of biting/stinging insects. My past field partners and supervisors are able to attest to my professionalism, high levels of energy and ability to learn new skills quickly. Although field work can be difficult at times, I pride myself on my ability to self-motivate and keep a positive attitude through the longest of workdays. I am someone who does not tire easily, who can ensure that their field work is efficient and their data collection is accurate. Fun fact about me: I am from Toronto and have always loved to visit High Park (depicted in map). Additional hobbies I have include, hiking, birding, swimming and running.
